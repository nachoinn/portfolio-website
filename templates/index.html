About Me

I live in Rosario, Argentina, I love to travel, meet people and new places.
I was interested and involved with computers from early age. Videogames, software, hardware, and the internet, are things that I always liked, spent hundreds of hours with them and wanting to know how they work, love to build PCs and repair them when needed.
From 2013 I started to get very interested in Bitcoin and how the blockchains work and some years later,
how the decentralized apps can change people's lives for the better.
I'm a hobby DJ and Music Producer since 2015, released a label signed house track in 2019 in spotify and beatport 'Nainn - Loading'.
I run a 3D printing business since 2020 offering solutions to clients and having fun designing/remixing parts.
Started to study web development with HTML and CSS in 2020, studied Python focused on cybersecurity scripts in 2021,
and since beginnings of 2022 I added web development, automation, web scraping and data science with Python.
